# Summary Report: “Vietnam Cloud Day 2025 – Ho Chi Minh City Connect Edition for Builders”

### General Introduction

**Vietnam Cloud Day 2025 – Ho Chi Minh City Connect Edition for Builders** was a **specialized livestream gathering** hosted by **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**. The program connected the **builder ecosystem in Ho Chi Minh City** with the **main conference in Hanoi**, allowing participants in the South to engage with cutting-edge updates on **cloud technologies, AI, and modernization practices**.  
This edition offered a collaborative space for **developers, architects, and tech leaders** to follow keynotes, technical sessions, and discussions directly broadcast from the capital.

---

### Event Objectives

- Strengthen the link between **AWS builders in Ho Chi Minh City** and speakers from the **Hanoi main event**.  
- Deliver the newest insights in **Cloud Computing, Generative AI, and Application Modernization**.  
- Share **real-world best practices** for building scalable, secure, and fault-tolerant cloud systems.  
- Promote **community engagement and professional networking** among cloud practitioners, startups, and organizations.  
- Encourage adoption of **modern architectural patterns** and the responsible use of **AI in production workloads**.

---

### List of Speakers

- **Conor McNamara** – Managing Director, AWS ASEAN  
- **Alois Reitbauer** – VP & Chief Technical Evangelist, Dynatrace  
- **Erica Liu** – Senior GTM Specialist, App Modernization, AWS  
- **Fabrianne Effendi** – Associate Specialist SA, Serverless, AWS  
- **Jignesh Shah** – Director, Open Source Databases, AWS  
- **Tran Nhat Quang** – Solutions Architect Lead, AWS Vietnam  
- **Nguyen Thi Thuy Linh** – Senior Partner Manager, AWS Vietnam  

*All speakers presented live in Hanoi, with their sessions streamed to the HCMC Connect Edition.*

---

### Key Highlights

#### 1. Opening Keynote (Livestream from Hanoi)
- Overview of **Vietnam’s cloud adoption progress** and AWS’s role in national digitalization.  
- Announcement of **AWS programs** supporting startups, SMBs, and educational institutions.  
- Introduction to **AWS Generative AI offerings**, focusing on secure, scalable, and practical AI adoption.

#### 2. Builders Session (Local Discussion)
- Deep dive into **Event-Driven Architecture (EDA)** and **Domain-Driven Design (DDD)** for cloud-native workloads.  
- Practical demonstrations on **modernizing microservices** with decoupling, scalability, and reliability in mind.  
- Showcase of **Amazon Q Developer** and how it enhances every stage of the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC).

#### 3. Networking & Community Interaction
- Direct conversations among **developers, builders, and AWS community leaders**.  
- Sharing experiences related to **cloud careers, certifications, and DevOps growth paths**.  
- Discussion of **AWS learning resources**, certification journeys, and the **AWS Community Builder Program**.

---
